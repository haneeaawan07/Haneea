Business Incubation Center – PAFIAST

Empowering Entrepreneurs for Tomorrow!

Established in 2023 with the support of the Higher Education Commission (HEC), Pakistan, the Business Incubation Center (BIC) at PAF-IAST is dedicated to fostering entrepreneurship in the region. Our mission is to provide aspiring entrepreneurs with the essential tools and resources needed to elevate their business ventures and contribute to the economic prosperity of the community.


About BIC

At BIC, PAF-IAST, we have developed a robust infrastructure to nurture and guide emerging entrepreneurs on their journey to success. Our primary goal is to promote sustainable business ventures with significant financial potential. The startups incubated within our center benefit from a range of services and resources geared towards their holistic business development.

Objectives

  • Promoting Self-Employment
    Committed to inspiring and empowering youth.
  • Improving Efficiency
    Targeted programs to enhance startup productivity.
  • Business Development
    Covering key areas in finance, marketing, and management.
  • Network Development
    Connecting startups with industry leaders and collaborators.

Services & Resources

  • Customized Consultancy
    Tailored services to address unique startup needs.
  • Educated Workforce
    Access to a pool of talented, innovative individuals.
  • Financial & Marketing Assistance
    Support securing funding and developing strategies.
  • Technological Expertise
    Specialized tech guidance to stay ahead.

About The Institute

Message from Rector

A dream come true for technical education.

Today, knowledge along with skill is the key difference between developed and underdeveloped economies. Pakistan lacks in adequately trained and skilled work force in production sector, keeping it behind others.

To become a developed economy, it is imperative for the nation to invest in and leverage its engineering sector. PAF-IAST is all set to change this rut through a conscious effort by tying practical skills and industry engagement to its programs.

Our Green Campus

Nestled in the natural mountains of Haripur.

PAF IAST is flanked by mountains from all sides, offering the ravishing view of snow-capped peaks of Nathiagali and natural winding trails. Set in the middle of the campus is a natural lake, fed by the springs of surrounding mountains.

The ideal setting is equally conducive to academic activity and aesthetic sensibilities, providing a serene island far from the chaos of metropolitan culture.

Skilling Pakistan

Collaborative venture with Austrian & Chinese Universities.

We aim to raise the standards of technical education in Pakistan at par with those of most advanced industrial economies. We are fully conscious of the need to link employment with education.

By harnessing high-quality manpower, we induce not only an inward flow of technology investment into Pakistan but also enable the generation of local hi-tech industry.
